Agridera Fresh Market Carrot Varieties

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of vegetable seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on developing high-yield carrot varieties with excellent color, taste and storage qualities. Our breeding programs focus on varieties resistant to common carrot diseases and adapted to a wide range of growing conditions.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is for earliness, different root sizes, firmness, deep orange color, flavor, crack resistance, shelf life, and high/low temperature adaptability.

Agridera Carrot breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse varieties, Open-field varieties, and Storage varieties.

Greenhouse Carrot
Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. Focus on early maturity, uniform root shape and resistances to main soil-borne diseases.

Open-field
Varieties

This breeding is for Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The main focus is on large roots with high carotene content and excellent storage qualities.

Storage
Varieties

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets. We breed varieties with excellent keeping quality, uniform shape and size, and resistance to splitting.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
